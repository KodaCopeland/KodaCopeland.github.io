About

Koda Copeland is an indie furry author born and raised in Oregon. His love of stories started at a young age when his parents bought him his first video camera. He continued to make short movies with his friends until college, where he majored in journalism, focusing on documentary production. By day, he works as a video producer and photographer for a marketing team.

As an avid reader of fiction, furry and otherwise, Copeland always dreamed of writing a novel. In 2020 he set out to make that a reality and, with that, found a new passion.

Copeland's first novel, My Remise released in March 2025.

Books

  • My Remise

    Wrestling is life to Artbe. At least it was until a disastrous match shattered his chances of transferring from his community college to a real-deal university wrestling team. When an offer from a mysterious figure promises to solve his problems, he finds himself thrust into Deavon, a world filled with talking animals called beasts. Tasked with joining a prestigious guard and stealing a magical codex from a tyrannical polar bear who has plunged the land into eternal winter, Artbe must navigate an unfamiliar society while putting together the broken pieces of his life.

    Complicating things further, Artbe finds himself attracted to his new roommate, a striking male wolf named Leofric. As the two grow closer, Artbe begins to question his sexuality and his place in life. If he has any hope of retrieving the codex and ending the permanent winter, he must learn not only what it means to be human, but also what it means to be a beast

  • The Morning After

    A post-transformation anthology featuring the short story From Tractor Sales to Fluffy Tails by Koda Copeland.

    Transformation is a story by itself. But what happens after the body has finished changing? What does life look like with a body no longer your own?

    "After celebrating the biggest sale of his career as a tractor salesman, Clay hooksup with a farmer in a rural Oregon town. Hungover, with little memory of his sexual encounter, Clay discovers he has transformed into a werewolf. Stuck shifted with the plans for his life now in question, Clay, along with the help of his one-night stand, seeks the town's Mayor for a cure."

    The Morning after includes additional stories by Sandy Andrews, Anhedral, Yana Caoránach, Deborah L. Davitt, Sasha Drachen, Wildeflit Dreams, Geo Holms, Eric Malves, Pão Pão, Lauren Rivers, Marshe Summers, Kewey Tanuki, Televassi, and Peter Vriens.
